wradlib.georef.get_earth_radius¶

wradlib.georef.get_earth_radius(latitude, sr=None)¶

Get the radius of the Earth (in km) for a given Spheroid model (sr) at a given position

R^2 = ( a^4 cos(f)^2 + b^4 sin(f)^2 ) / ( a^2 cos(f)^2 + b^2 sin(f)^2 ).

Parameters:
  • sr (osr object) – spatial reference;
  • latitude (float) – geodetic latitude in degrees;
Returns:

radius (float) – earth radius in meter
