correctAttenuationHB Gate-by-Gate attenuation correction according to
correctAttenuationKraemer Gate-by-Gate attenuation correction according to [Kraemer et al., 2008].
correctAttenuationHJ Gate-by-Gate attenuation correction based on [Kraemer et al., 2008], expanded by [Jacobi et al., 2012].
constraint_dBZ Constraint callback function for correctAttenuationConstrained.
constraint_pia Constraint callback function for correctAttenuationConstrained.
correctAttenuationConstrained2 Gate-by-Gate attenuation correction based on the iterative approach of [Kraemer et al., 2008] with a generalized and scalable number of constraints.
correctRadomeAttenuationEmpirical Estimate two-way wet radome losses as an empirical function of frequency and rainfall rate for both standard and hydrophobic radomes based on the approach of [Merceret et al., 2000].
pia_from_kdp Retrieving path integrated attenuation from specific differential phase (Kdp).
